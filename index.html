About me
I am a passionate Full Stack Developer with a strong background in Control Engineering and Industrial Automation. My expertise includes developing innovative web applications using Java, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, alongside programming PLCs and designing control systems. I thrive in collaborative environments and agile settings, where I can leverage my technical skills to deliver impactful solutions that meet client needs.

I am particularly interested in roles that involve designing state-of-the-art electronic and DDC control systems for building automation. My goal is to contribute to projects that optimize HVAC operations and enhance overall system performance.
